## Google Indexing Steps

1. Publish the site publicly with HTTPS.
2. Open Google Search Console and add the domain or URL-prefix property.
3. Verify ownership.
4. Submit `https://michels-lighting-industries.com/sitemap.xml`.
5. Use URL Inspection for the homepage and request indexing.
6. Make sure `robots.txt` is reachable at `https://michels-lighting-industries.com/robots.txt`.
7. Search for the page after indexing with queries like:
   - `Casimir Michel`
   - `Casimir Michel Mettmann`
   - `Casimir Veranstaltungstechnik`
   - `Michel Veranstaltungstechnik`
   - `Michels Lighting Industries`
